    Program.cs:
        Entry point of the application.
        Handles initialization and setup.
        Contains the main loop for user interaction.

    PasswordManager.cs:
        Core logic for managing passwords.
        Includes methods for adding, retrieving, updating, and deleting passwords.
        Interacts directly with the EncryptionHelper and PasswordStorage.

    EncryptionHelper.cs:
        Handles encryption and decryption of passwords.
        Provides methods for encrypting and decrypting passwords.

    PasswordStorage.cs:
        Manages the storage of password entries.
        May use a simple file-based storage mechanism for simplicity.
